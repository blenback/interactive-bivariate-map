Identifying optimal locations for conservation planning in Switzerland under uncertain future change

Robustness of Ecosystem Service provision and Biodiversity under a spectrum of future conditions in the Canton of Bern

How to interpret
X-axis: Performance I Performance is calculated on a per-pixel basis as the mean of the cumulative sum of the change in ES and/or BD over all future time points for all scenarios. The use of the cumulative sum captures both positive and negative changes, thus a high value of Performance indicates that pixels generally display positive changes in ES and/or BD across all scenarios.

Low on the left, high on the right.

Y-axis: Stability I Stability is calculated using the Undesirable Deviation Metric, which sums the deviation (regret) away from the median value of the cumulative change in ES and/or BD for all instances below the median. In simple terms, it captures the extent of negative variation across scenarios, with higher values indicating greater variation.

Low at the bottom, high at the top.

Grey pixels are those that displayed low performance and low stability, while Dark Green were those that displayed high performance and high stability.

Gold indicates low performance but high stability, while blue indicates high performance but low stability.
